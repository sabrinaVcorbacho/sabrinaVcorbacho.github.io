About

A hybrid profile across research, data, consulting, and AI.

My background connects political science, computational social science, public opinion research, consulting, teaching, and AI evaluation. I am especially interested in how political behavior, media environments, and local institutional contexts shape how people express opinions.

I hold an MS in Data Analytics & Computational Social Science from UMass Amherst and a Licenciatura in Political Science from Universidad del CEMA. My work focuses on Latin America, subnational politics, public opinion, political expression, media framing, and AI as a tool for social research.
